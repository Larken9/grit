Membership FAQ’s

How do guest passes work? +

You receive two guest passes per month; guests must be accompanied by a member.

Can I change my home club? +

You can switch clubs once per quarter through your online account.

Do you offer family memberships? +

Yes—our family plan covers two adults and up to three children under 18. Contact support for pricing.

What COVID-19 measures are in place? +

We enforce enhanced cleaning, reduced capacity, and require masks in all common areas.

Can I bring a guest to group classes? +

Guests are welcome but must register 24 hours in advance and pay a one-time drop-in fee.
